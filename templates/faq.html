Bharat Vault : The Crypto Wallet - FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Bharat Vault is a secure crypto wallet designed to help you manage and send Litecoin safely and efficiently.

Simply click the "Create Wallet" button on the homepage and follow the step-by-step instructions to set up your wallet securely.

After setting up your wallet, navigate to the "Send Litecoin" section, enter the recipient's address and amount, and confirm your transaction.

Your security is our top priority. We use advanced encryption and two-factor authentication to ensure that your data and funds remain safe.

If you forget your password, please contact our support team immediately. For security reasons, we cannot retrieve your password, but we can help with account recovery.
